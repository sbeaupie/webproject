Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions here.

1. How To Design Your Own Artwork or Clothes?

We provide a platform where you can collaborate with our designers to create your own artwork or clothing designs. Simply contact us to set up a consultation. We will guide you through the process of selecting materials, colors, and themes that bring your creative ideas to life.

2. Do We Do Promotions or Fundraisers?

Yes, we do! We often partner with organizations for fundraising events and offer promotional discounts during special occasions. If you are interested in collaborating with us for a promotion or fundraiser, please reach out via our contact page for more information.

3. Do We Do Delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery services for all orders. We partner with trusted courier services to ensure your items reach you safely and on time. You can choose between standard and express delivery options during checkout.

4. Do We Have In-Store Selling?

Currently, we do not have a physical store for direct purchases. All our products are available online through our website. However, we sometimes participate in pop-up events, so keep an eye on our social media for updates on upcoming events.

5. Can We Make Custom Designs?

Absolutely! Custom design is one of our specialties. You can work directly with our design team to create unique pieces that reflect your personal style. Whether it's clothing, hoodies, or accessories, we love bringing your vision to life. Contact us to get started!
